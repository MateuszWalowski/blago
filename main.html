EXPERIMENTS

It's a kind of magic...

I am an artist from the bottom of my heart, in every bone and every thought, my imagination has no limits and everything around me is material for work or inspiration. I like to see happy and smiling faces and that's why I like working with people and opening new spaces for them.
My art is related to nature and I want to help people to get close to the nature, to feel it better, to connect with their roots. I love to use natural materials.
In my artworks and designs I am merging my educations in art, graphic design, fashion design and ecology.
